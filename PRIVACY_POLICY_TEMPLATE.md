# Privacy Policy for FIRECalc

**Last Updated:** February 20, 2026

## Introduction

FIRECalc ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our iOS application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
FIRECalc stores the following information **locally on your device**:
- Portfolio assets (names, quantities, prices, asset classes)
- Simulation parameters and results
- User preferences and settings
- Retirement planning inputs (age, savings, spending goals)
- Performance tracking snapshots
- Pension and guaranteed income information

### Information We Do NOT Collect
- We do not collect any personal information (name, email, phone number)
- We do not track your location
- We do not access your contacts, photos, or other device data
- We do not require an account or login
- We do not use cookies or tracking technologies

## How We Use Your Information

All data you enter into FIRECalc is:
- **Stored locally** on your device only
- **Never transmitted** to our servers (we don't have any!)
- **Never shared** with third parties
- **Under your complete control**

The only exception is if you enable **iCloud sync** (optional):
- Data is synchronized across your devices using Apple's iCloud service
- Data is encrypted by Apple and subject to Apple's privacy policy
- We never have access to your iCloud data

## Third-Party Services

### Price Data
FIRECalc uses Yahoo Finance's public API to fetch live price data for publicly traded assets:
- Only ticker symbols are sent to Yahoo Finance
- No personal or portfolio information is transmitted
- Price data is cached locally to minimize requests
- You can use the app without live price updates if preferred

### Historical Market Data
The app includes 100 years of historical market data (1926-2024) bundled within the app:
- This data is stored locally and never transmitted anywhere
- Used for Monte Carlo simulations
- Sourced from public academic research and government data

## Data Storage and Security

- All data is stored in your device's secure app sandbox
- If you enable iCloud sync, Apple handles encryption and security
- Your financial information never leaves your control
- No passwords or sensitive authentication data is stored

## Your Rights and Choices

You have complete control over your data:

### Data Access
- All your data is accessible within the app
- You can export your portfolio to JSON format at any time

### Data Deletion
- Delete individual assets or entire portfolios anytime
- Clear all data via Settings → Clear All Data
- Uninstalling the app removes all local data

### iCloud Sync (Optional)
- Enable or disable in Settings
- Managed by Apple's iCloud service
- Subject to Apple's privacy policy

## Children's Privacy

FIRECalc is not intended for use by children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

## Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Changes will be posted here with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.

## No Data Breaches Possible

Since we don't collect, store, or transmit your personal information to any servers, there is no risk of a data breach on our end. Your data stays on your device or in your personal iCloud account.

## Financial Disclaimer

**Important:** FIRECalc provides estimates for educational purposes only. Results are based on historical data and assumptions that may not reflect future performance. Always consult with a qualified financial advisor before making investment decisions.

Past performance does not guarantee future results. Market simulations are probabilistic and cannot predict actual outcomes.
